# Implementation Plan: Free Tier Child Account Limits

**Branch**: `028-child-account-limits` | **Date**: 2026-03-06 | **Spec**: [spec.md](./spec.md)
**Input**: Feature specification from `/specs/028-child-account-limits/spec.md`

## Summary

Limit free-tier families to 2 active child accounts by introducing a new `is_disabled` boolean on the `children` table. Children beyond the 2nd on a free tier are created as disabled — blocked from login, transactions, allowance, and interest. The ChildSelectorBar shows disabled children with muted styling and an upgrade CTA. Subscription webhook handlers enable all children on upgrade and disable excess children on downgrade.
